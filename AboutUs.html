Chapter 1 - Genesis

It's October 2023... LoanBox is born...

Started as a Just an Idea...
very soon morphed into an Future plan -
To Create the first easy loan device
in the world.

At first, one person worked on the project.
Nothing was planned.

It was entertaining... not bad...
but nothing special... until
The first publish.

Chapter 2 - Process

The process of realizing the idea
has been ongoing since October
2023 and continues until the startup
is fully formed.

Currently, one person is involved in the project,
but in the future, many more
people will join the team.

Chapter 3 - Initial reaction

For the first time,
Relatives heard about LoanBox,
for whom this idea was quite original
and interesting.

Nevertheless, they noticed some flaws
in the idea of ​​LoanBox and
contributed a lot to its perfection.

In short, we can say that
LoanBox was created by the community.
